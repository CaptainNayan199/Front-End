In these level we will be looking at box model including; padding, margin, border.

Box Model - Central concept in CSS that otlines the design and layout of elements on the web page.

Box Model components : Consists of 4 main components - margin, border, padding, and content

Margin - The space outside the border, seperating the element from others.

Border - The outline that encapsulates the padding and content

Padding : The space between the border and the actual content, providing a buffer.

Content - Th innermost layer where text, images, or other media are housed.
